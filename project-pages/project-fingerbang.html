Fingerbang: All Bullets Pointin'
A fast-paced FPS inspired by both retro and modern FPS games. Fingerbang combines elements from each in order to create a mix of its own, with a scoring system added on top to gauge your performance through the different levels.
Project Details
Project Fingerbang began its life when the initial concepting phase of Ticorporate started on September of 2022. I had thought up the original concept some time earlier, and suggested it as a basis for further refinement for my partner-in-crime Aleksi Pellinen. After additional ideation and a quick two-week gameplay prototype later, we ended up among the few concepts selected for further production.
Very basic mechanics in place such as shooting.
The early prototype also featured parrying and dashing.
This very early prototype was created in two weeks by me, using a mix of proprietary graphical assets made by Aleksi and premade textures. Due to time constraints the prototype level was put together using Unity's default cubes. The entire prototype consists of only 12 simple scripts, mostly allowing for basic interactions between the player and enemies. This would however end up serving as the basis upon which the eventual project would be built.
Some scripts and prefab predecessors would be carried over to the final product.
At the beginning of pre-production, we as a team made some clear guidelines for ourselves going forward, such as setting in stone the scale for objects in the project (1 unit in Unity would roughly correspond to one meter, and every "tile" would be 4x4 units in size for example). Almost immediately afterwards I began working on our first level, the initial plan for which was to only cover the basic mechanics of the game and immediately lead into the main level planned for the game.
